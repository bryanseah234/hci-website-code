Language Elective Programme (Chinese)

语文特选课程（华文）

      华中在1990年推出语文特选课程（简称语特），旨在陶铸一批通晓双语而又对中华文化有所认识的双语精英。自课程设立以来，华中学生的表现非常出色，每年都有超过半数学生获得教育部颁发的语文特选课程奖学金。

      从1991年起，有无数名语特毕业生荣获奖学金，远赴中国、台湾、英国、美国、日本、澳洲、法国和德国等地著名大学深造，攻读不同的专业。迄今为止，华中语特已造就了两名极具荣誉的总统奖学金得主（1994年和2002年）以及为数不少的总理书籍奖得主。历届语特毕业生学成之后，投入文教界、传媒界、司法界、外交界、财经界和政军界，在各个领域扮演举足轻重的角色。

Since its inception in 1990, the Hwa Chong Chinese Language Elective Programme (CLEP) has dedicated itself to nurturing the nation’s next generation of bilingual talents and cultural ambassadors, well-versed in the intricacies of the Chinese Language and Culture.

CLEP students have continually surpassed expectations, with over half of the annual student population being recipients of the prestigious LEP Scholarship, awarded by the Ministry of Education.

From 1991, numerous 130 CLEP students-cum-scholarship awardees have been given opportunities to further their studies in not only China and Taiwan but also the United Kingdom, the United States of America, Japan, Australia and even France and Germany, among other European countries.

To date, 2 CLEP graduates have been conferred the President’s Scholarship (1994, 2002), while others have garnered the outstanding Prime Minister’s Book Prize, an annual award conferred on the top bilingual students of each cohort at the primary, secondary and pre-university levels.

Upon graduation, these bilingual talents have moved on to further their careers in various fields such as the Education, Humanities, Law, Foreign Affairs, Media, Finance, Defence, Public Service and Politics where they continue to excel and contribute to society.
