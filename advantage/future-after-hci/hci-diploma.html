HCI Diploma

The HCI Diploma is welcomed by 19 top universities across the world. Read more about our diploma by clicking on the left menu, or in the Student Development Awards section.


HCI Diploma

In 2008, we also launched the Hwa Chong Diploma.  To date, 4 local universities, namely, NTU, NUS, SMU & SUTD, and many top universities including Brown, Chicago, Columbia, Duke, Harvard, Yale, Imperial College London, King’s College London, London School of Economics and Warwick welcome our diploma for admission purposes. 

Since 2009, China’s top three universities – Peking, Tsinghua and Fudan – have awarded Hwa Chong students with full-scholarships based on the strength of the Hwa Chong Diploma.
