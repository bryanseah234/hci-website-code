LIVE WITH PASSION
LEAD WITH COMPASSION

Through the years, Hwa Chong Institution has nurtured leaders for the nation in the highest echelons of research, industry and government. Our aim is to maximise every student’s potential, and to prepare them for life in the new global age.

School Motto


Best of East And West

At the heart of the Hwa Chong programme is the emphasis we place on moral and character education. The best of Chinese values and Western ideas that have had a strong impact on society are infused into our school curriculum and culture.

  • Campuses in China
  • Partnership with Columbia University and
    Scarsdale Public Schools (New York)
  • Bilingual Leaders

Passion-Driven Learning

At Hwa Chong, students enjoy a holistic education that puts them in touch with the dynamics and challenges of the real world. Every Hwa Chong student receives a rigorous curriculum that emphasises leadership, character building, academic excellence and creativity.

  • iSpark (School Based Gifted Education Programme)
  • Talent Programmes for high-achievers in Mathematics & Sciences (SMTP & GATE)
  • Humanities Programme
  • Bicultural Studies Programme
  • Language Elective Programme (Chinese)
  • Art Elective
  • H3 Programmes
  • Research (CENTAD)

Character And Leadership

We want to engender passion and compassion in Hwa Chong students; to hone them into leaders who are not only responsible to self, family and country, but who can also help sustain humanity and transform society.

  • Leadership Education
  • Guidance Education
  • Building Character

Peaks of Excellence

A commitment to excellence is a hallmark of Hwa Chong, whose mission is to nurture leaders for the nation. Besides producing some of the nation’s top scholars at the GCE A- Level Exams, our students have also brought pride to Singapore and the school with 60 World Championship titles from 2006-2020.

  • 60 World Champs
  • Academic Excellence
  • Arts and Culture Excellence
  • School Excellence
  • Sports Victories

At the Forefront of Innovation

HCI was featured as one of the World’s Most Innovative Schools in 2009 by the South China Morning Post, Hong Kong’s premier English daily. With exciting initiatives in education, HCI is constantly at the frontier of educational change and innovation..

  • Integrated Programme
  • Science Research Centre
  • Beijing Satellite Campus
  • HCI Diploma
  • Hwa Chong Integrated Boarding Programme

Our Hwa Chong Family

The vibrant Hwa Chong community is a close-knit one, with school leaders, staff, teachers, students and alumni coming together to form one large Hwa Chong Family.
